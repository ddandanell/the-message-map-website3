## Quick Data Collection Tips

### Finding Information Efficiently:
1. **Google Maps Profile** has most basic info (hours, phone, website)
2. **Photos tab** - download 5-6 photos per business
3. **Reviews tab** - sort by "Most relevant" and read 10-20 reviews
4. **About section** - check for services, amenities, attributes
5. **Business website** - verify prices, services, booking options

### Priority Ranking Criteria:
- **High priority:** 4.5+ stars with 200+ reviews
- **Medium priority:** 4.0+ stars with 100-200 reviews
- **Consider:** 4.5+ stars with 50-100 reviews (newer but strong)
- **Skip:** Under 4.0 stars or less than 30 reviews

### Red Flags to Avoid:
- Businesses marked as "Permanently closed"
- Many recent negative reviews mentioning harassment or scams
- Extremely low prices that seem suspicious
- Listings without photos or minimal information
